Welcome to The Ugly Crossiant

This is the home of all of my eggless recipes both sweet and savoury alike. I have designed these to be delicious, whether you choose to eat eggs or not, so please dive straight in.

A little about myself

My name is Nicola Easton, a 20 year old student from the UK and I am the author of The Ugly Croissant. Baking has been a passion of mine since I was very small and I have grown up testing all of the recipes I can get my hands on, so I thought it was about time that I share some of mine.

I started baking eggless recipes about two years ago when I met my boyfriend who has an egg allergy. It’s been an uphill struggle trying to adapt and create recipes, with a few headaches along the way, but so far its been generally successful! This website is here to document my attempts at creating all kinds of delicious goods with a recipe page for all of my best bakes and a blog section on the homepage for comments on slightly less triumphant attempts.

The biggest shame I found with eggless cooking is that there are very limited resources online or in books. I want this website to try and fill a gap for those looking for recipes, whether you’re vegetarian, intolerant or just wanting to try some delicious treats.

To find all of my posts, experiments and recipe announcements, check out my Instagram account @theuglycroissant.

Why the name?

My dad called my croissants ugly, it caught on.
